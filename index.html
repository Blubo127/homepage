Biography

I am a first-year PhD student in the joint program between Zhejiang University and Westlake University (2025—), advised by Prof. Yue Zhang. My current research interests focus on Multimodal Learning and LLM-based Agents. Alongside my research, I am actively working on several innovation and entrepreneurship projects. For a complete list of my publications, please visit my Google Scholar profile.

Before starting my PhD, I worked as a research assistant at Westlake University (2023—2025). I received my M.Eng. from Tianjin University (2020—2023), advised by Asst. Prof. Meishan Zhang, and my B.Sc. from Huazhong Agricultural University (2016—2020).

Utilized UAV to capture images of a simulation environment, then used image descriptions to direct the UAV's flight to a specific position. Implemented a pixel-based technique to determine distance by tracking image pixel changes.
