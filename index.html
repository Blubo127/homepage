Biography

I am Pan-zhong Lu, and I am pursuing a suitable PHD position for 2025Spring/Fall. Now, I am a research assistant at Westlake University, my mentor is Prof. Yue Zhang. My research interests include Multimodal, LLM, Machine Learning, Robotics, Embodied AI, etc. I am open to any meaningful research topics during my doctoral studies. Before that, I did my master at Tianjin University (2020-2023), where I was advised by Asst. Prof. Meishan Zhang. I did my bachelor at Huazhong Agricultural University (2016-2020).

Selected Projects

Customized Virtual Digital Human, 2023

Employed innovative Large Language Model, Automatic Speech Recognition, Text-to-Speech, and Talking Head Generation technologies to develop interactive, tailor-made virtual digital personas. [Demo]

Students Online Quiz System, 2023

Independently designed and implemented an Online Quiz System using Django, which has been deployed on a cloud server for teachers to conduct their classes and students to take tests. [Link]

Human-computer Interaction in Simulation Environment, 2022

Utilized UAV to capture images of simulation environment, then used image descriptions to direct the UAV's flight to a specific position. Implemented a pixel-based technique to determine distance by tracking image pixel changes. [Demo]

Visual Language Navigation in Real Environment, 2021

Explored visual language navigation for UAV in real environment using VLNCE model. Predicted user instructions and RGB/depth images transmitted to UAV via ROS for corresponding actions. [Code]
